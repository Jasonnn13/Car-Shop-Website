Vision

To be the foremost destination for automotive enthusiasts in
Indonesia, renowned for our commitment to luxury, innovation, and
unparalleled customer satisfaction.

Mission

Curate a meticulously selected collection of premium vehicles that
embody the pinnacle of automotive craftsmanship and innovation
and foster lasting relationships with our customers through
comprehensive after-sales support, ensuring their ownership
experience is nothing short of exceptional.

History

DiamondCut Motors was founded in 1998 by a team of automotive
enthusiasts with a shared vision of redefining the automotive retail
experience in Indonesia. From humble beginnings, our showroom
has grown to become a premier destination for luxury automotive
aficionados, renowned for our curated selection of premium vehicles
and unparalleled customer service.
